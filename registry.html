Our Registry

We are going to be living in the US for the next few years, so we have set up a couple registries that ship to our church's address in the US. As our apartment is quite small, and we will be moving back to New Zealand after my PhD is finished, we want to avoid having too much clutter around. Each registry has a list of things that we would find really useful, but feel free to be inventive as well!

Each registry has a list of specific items that we need, but we have a few other ideas as well. We have enough tea towels to dry the kitchen more than once, so don't worry about that! Email us if you want to help out in any other way, otherwise we look forward to seeing you on our wedding day. Thank you!
