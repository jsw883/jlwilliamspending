Her

Laura Holtslag

I'm Laura, and I am engaged to that guy you see over there! I live in NZ with my family, and have just completed my final year of school. I am currently working at a sewing shop, and studying childcare / child-development and will hopefully become a qualified nanny! I have lived in the US for three years, and am looking forward to moving back there with James, and meeting all of his US friends!

I am incredibly happy to have James at my side, and so excited to begin the next chapter in the story of our lives!
