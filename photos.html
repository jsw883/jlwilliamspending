    Engaged

    On the first Sunday after I got back for Christmas, we went for a walk in the botanical gardens and visited the spot where Laura agreed to marry me! It was a beautiful summer day, and Grandma was with us, armed with her camera as usual. Laura was sitting on the bench with all the bird poo when I snuck up behind her to pop the question, and I was desperately trying not to step on any branches!

      Party

      Even though I was only back for three weeks over the northern hemisphere winter holidays, we managed to have our engagement party on the last night before I was due to return, and we were humbled by how many of our friends came from Burnside High School, Middleton Grange High School, No. 17 Squadron Air Training Corps, the University of Canterbury, and the Reformed Churches of Christchurch! Thank you all for coming and for you thoughtful engagement presents!
